About the AU

Everything Has Changed is an HP AU that focuses mainly on the hypothesis that Hermione Granger is secretly Sirius Black and Marlene McKinnon's daughter. EHC is a multi-ship AU, although in most interactions is Fred x Hermione. The main changes from canon are that Hermione is a year older, Fred and George are one year younger and Ginny and Ron are twins, and Hermione's childhood - which is never explicitly described in canon. From the beginning of the 1st year the story progresses very differently.
